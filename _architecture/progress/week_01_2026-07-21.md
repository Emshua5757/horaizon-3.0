## Decisions Made

1. **Native-first over SDUI** — see `_architecture/decisions/ADR-001_native_over_sdui.md`
2. **HBP v2 = data-only MessagePack over WebSocket** — no JSON, no HTTP, no REST
3. **Governor is always-on** — never sleeps, manages all other processes
4. **One model at a time in Ollama** — 4 GB RAM cap on Pi5, evict before load
5. **SIGSTOP/SIGCONT for module power management** — cgroups v2 for RAM isolation
